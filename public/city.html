PITTSBURGH

We <3 Pittsburgh.

Pittsburgh is an awesome city with a lot of history: it was a fort in the early days of the US, it was a hub of the steel industry, and it's now becoming a center of technology, education, and health care. We have many active technology user groups, startups and incubators, and headquarters of large companies. Pittsburgh is also generally cheaper to travel to and stay in than larger cities like New York City or San Francisco.

Our "ulterior" motive of the conference is to show off our city as the beautiful, affordable, and fun place it is. We'd love to to feed the growth of the area and make more people aware of the opportunities here! To that end, at Steel City Ruby Conf you will experience a showcase of local Pittsburgh speakers, sponsors, food, and activities.
